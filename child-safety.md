# Free Armenian Child Safety & Account Deletion Policy

## Child Safety Standards

Free Armenian has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We prohibit any content, activity, or communication involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.

Our platform is committed to:

* Removing prohibited content.
* Providing users with a way to report violations.
* Cooperating with law enforcement where legally required.
* Taking action against accounts that violate these standards.

To report child safety concerns, contact:

**Email:** [info@freearmenian.org](mailto:info@freearmenian.org)

---

## Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

To request deletion, contact:

**Email:** [info@freearmenian.org](mailto:info@freearmenian.org)

Please include the email address associated with your account.

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable time, except where data must be retained for legal, fraud prevention, or security purposes.
