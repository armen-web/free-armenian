Child Safety Standards

Free Armenian is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We maintain zero tolerance for any content or behavior involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.

  • Removal of prohibited content.
  • User reporting tools.
  • Account suspension or removal for violations.
  • Cooperation with competent law enforcement authorities where required by law.

To report child safety concerns please contact:

Email: info@freearmenian.org

Account & Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Free Armenian account and associated personal data at any time.

To request account deletion please contact:

Email: info@freearmenian.org

Please include the email address associated with your account.

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period except where information must be retained for legal, fraud prevention or security purposes.

Privacy

Free Armenian is committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal information responsibly.
